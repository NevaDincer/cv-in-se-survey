## Notes

Physical robot drives touch screen testing without any framework injection. Records human interactions from video into replayable test scripts via CV. Earlier and simpler version of the [[Image_Processing-Enabled_Validation_for_Robotic_Test_Automat]] line of work. ICSE 2020, strong multi-device evaluation.
